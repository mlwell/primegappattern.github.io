What is interesting is the pattern of holes. Conclusion or conjectures of this pattern
1. Gaps of 2 + 6n (n=0, 1,2 ...) will never have next gaps of 2 + 6n, except gaps between 3,5,7
2. Gaps of 4 + 6n (n=0, 1,2 ...) will never have next gaps of 4 + 6n
3. There is no consecutive twin gaps except 3,5,7
